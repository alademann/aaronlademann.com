Summary:

As you can see, Pinkel and Co. have continued to improve the overall quality of the talent. Even though many "whiffs" can be pointed out, a points system like RR is a great way to look at overall success for a recruiting class. This year, the 2009 class has been bemoaned by many on recruiting message boards - but really without a solid foundation in reality. We can still take a bigger step forward, but we're not "falling off", either. All of Pinkel's recruiting classes must be taken with a grain of salt as their recruiting evaluation methods tend to dig up more 2* gems rather than taking a chance on a bunch of 4* head cases.

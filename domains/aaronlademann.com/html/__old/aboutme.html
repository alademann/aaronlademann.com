My Company - Fpweb.net Managed Hosting

I am the lead interface developer for Amp Technology - the parent company of the SharePoint Hosting Pioneer - Fpweb.net.

Fpweb.net is a Microsoft Gold Certified team of I.T. experts in providing managed web hosting services for Windows SharePoint Server, Microsoft Office SharePoint Server, Microsoft Exchange, Microsoft Dynamics CRM 4.0 and dedicated server solutions to customers around the world. As one of the largest independent, U.S.-owned, managed hosting providers today, we serve companies ranging from the Fortune 500 to the SME (small and medium-enterprise) market. Our unmatched client list includes Boeing, Intel, The U.S. Air Force, and The New York Yankees all rely on Fpweb.net for their fully-managed Microsoft Enterprise Solutions.

Fpweb.net provides On Demand Expertise™. We strive to proactively fulfill even the unexpressed wishes and needs of our customers. It is this culture that sets us apart from our competitors; an intangible quality that keeps our valued customers intact and drives us to deliver the best service in the industry.
